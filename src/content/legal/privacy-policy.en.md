# Aido Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2026-02-10

Aido ("Company", "we", "us") complies with applicable privacy laws and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how we handle it.

## 1. Personal Data We Collect

We may collect the following information to provide our services:

- Account and sign-in data: email, nickname, profile image
- Service usage data: to-do data, friend connection data, device and log information
- Customer support data: inquiry content and contact details
- Subscription and payment-related data: subscription status, payment status, receipt verification data

## 2. Why We Process Personal Data

We process personal data only for the purposes below:

- User identification and account management
- Core features including task management, friend sharing, and achievement badges
- Service quality improvements and error response
- Subscription billing, settlement, and history management
- Customer support and service notices

## 3. AI Feature Data Processing

Aido may process data as follows to provide AI-powered recommendations and assistance:

- Some task text and context may be sent to external AI APIs to generate responses.
- External model services may include Gemini 2.0 Flash.
- Data is processed for AI response generation purposes only, and is not reused for advertising without consent.
- Detailed processing behavior of external AI services follows each provider's policy.

## 4. Third-Party Services and Data Processing

We do not provide personal data to third parties without user consent unless required by law. However, we may use external services that process data as needed for service operations.

- Infrastructure, analytics, authentication: Firebase, Google, Apple
- Payment and subscription: Apple App Store, Google Play, RevenueCat
- AI features: Google AI API (e.g., Gemini)

These providers process data under their own terms and privacy policies.

## 5. Data Retention Period

We retain personal data only as long as needed for the purposes described above, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

- Records regarding contracts or withdrawal requests: as required by applicable law
- Payment and transaction records: as required by applicable law
- Complaint and dispute handling records: as required by applicable law

## 6. Data Deletion

- Deletion procedure: data is deleted after retention expiration or when processing purpose is fulfilled
- Deletion method: electronic files are securely removed and paper records are shredded or incinerated

## 7. User Rights

Users may request:

- Access, correction, or deletion of personal data
- Restriction of processing
- Account withdrawal

Requests can be made via the contact email below. We process requests in accordance with applicable law.

## 8. Security Measures

We apply technical and organizational safeguards, including:

- Access control and minimum privilege management
- Access logging and monitoring
- Security updates and vulnerability checks
- Encryption for data transmission where applicable
